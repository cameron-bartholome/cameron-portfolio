An Excel-based decision logging tool designed to support structured engineering decision-making.

The tool captures design choices, assumptions, constraints, alternatives, and rationale in a clear and traceable format. It was developed to improve consistency and accountability in technical decisions, particularly during concept development and iterative design phases.
